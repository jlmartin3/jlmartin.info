LPRC Safer Communities Dashboard

In collaboration with the Gainesville Police Department (GPD) and Ersi inc., the Loss Prevention Research Council (LPRC) created an up-to-date crime incident map for the city to launch the SaferCommunities research initiative. Calls for service data are piped into ArcGIS online and visualized as a heat map along with LPRC StoreLab locations and field assets.

Project link: https://lprc.maps.arcgis.com/apps/dashboards/48060345419542659a23ff07d7f91e35
