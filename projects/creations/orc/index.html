LPRC Organized Retail Crime Survey

The Loss Prevention Research Council (LPRC), Loss Prevention Foundation (LPF), and Loss Prevention Magazine (LPM) conducted an industry-wide survey of investigators and multi-store loss prevention managers to understand the causes, consequences, and control of Organized Retail Crime (ORC) across the United States. My role was to assist in summarizing and communicating the results using a Tableau dashboard.

Project link: https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/loss.prevention.research.council/viz/ORC_Survey_2022/DashboardCatagories
